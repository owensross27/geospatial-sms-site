Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What we collect:

  • Phone number: Your phone number is used to receive and respond to your messages. It is stored temporarily for session management (active sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity).
  • Message content: Your text messages are processed to determine intent (e.g., transit directions, place search) and are not stored beyond the active session.
  • Location data: When you reference a location (e.g., "near union sq"), we geocode it to provide relevant results. We do not track or store your physical location.
  • Saved aliases: If you save a location alias (e.g., "save home as 284 Putnam Ave"), that alias is stored in association with your phone number so you can use it in future sessions.

What we do NOT do:

  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your phone number or data to third parties.
  • We do not send marketing or promotional messages.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not retain message content after your session expires.

Third-party services: We use Google Maps APIs (for geocoding, directions, and place search) and MTA data feeds (for real-time arrivals). Your queries are sent to these services in anonymized form (coordinates and search terms only, not your phone number).

Data deletion: Reply STOP to end your session and opt out. To request deletion of any saved aliases, email owensross27@gmail.com.
